Typography

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. By defining the correct typeface, line height and letter spacing the site will be easier to navigate, have higher click through rates, and increase the overall readability and aesthetics of the website.

Garamond Pro

Garamond Pro is a serif font that has been refined to work across a wide set of supported platforms.

We believe that Garamond Pro will be most effective used in the Title treatment with all uppercase letter casing.

Century Gothic

Lack of ascenders and descenders in all caps serif fonts make it harder to read. Additionally, overuse of the casing can feel like you're shouting at the user. Pairing Garamond Pro with the sans-serif, Century Gothic will help balance our typographic system.

Century Gothic in title case will be used for section titles and body text.

Treatment

By determining the correct line height and letter spacing you will be able to achieve proper readability and appropriate pacing. It is important to not only consider the line height between text with the same style, but text with different styles as well. The correct line height will also make the information more inviting to the viewer rather than intimidating.
