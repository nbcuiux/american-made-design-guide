Introduction

The American Made movie site will be a hub where movie goers can watch trailers, buy tickets and explore content of the movie.

Our goal is to give the visitors an immersive cinematic experience on the web that will entice and delight the audience to go and see the movie.

The purpose of this Design Guide is a way for us to document and communicate the design direction for American Made’s website, www.AmericanMadeMovie.net.
