Iconography

Iconography is used in place of text or with text to emphasis an idea. Over time viewers learn to recognize icons and associate actions or ideas with them. For American Made the use of simple line iconography will not only ensure the readability of the icons, but the longevity of them as well.

Contents

Line Icons

By using simple line iconography we can create a clean and modern aesthetic reinforcing the American Made brand. Adding a slight double drop shadow will make them pop over a busy background.

Logotype

We created an logotype to recreate the feeling from the American Made trailer. We went through a few cycles to hone in on the look and feel.

We used on a thinner and sharper plane in the type that feels like "a hilt of a sword".
