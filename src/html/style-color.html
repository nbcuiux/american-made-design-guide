Color

Color will be used to invoke emotion as well as guide the viewer through the different sections of the American Made film site. With the use of contrast and tones the viewer will be able to focus on the content while feeling an emotional connection to it. This will provide the viewer with a seamless experience while navigating through AmericanMadeMovie.com

Color Palette

By incorporting American colors into the color palette we can invoke feeling nationalism while preventing the film from feeling like a "period piece".

Tones

By lightening and darkening select colors of our palette we can achieve blacks and whites that pair well with the rest of our palette.

Vibrancy

To keep with the feeling, we have taken an image a red filter and various film grains to it. Dirty film grains hint at the era of the film and suggest at the grittyness and action-packed nature of the film.
